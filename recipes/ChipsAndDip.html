Chips and Dip

Description

Chips and dip is perfect for snacking or for a group eating item for friends and parties.

Ingredients

  • Chips
  • French Onion Dip
  • Bowl

Steps:

  • Step 1: Grab a bowl, jar of french onion dip, and your favorite dipping chips.
  • Step 2: Scoop the french onion dip into the bowl.
  • Step 3: Open the bag of chips, scoop into the dip for desired amount.
  • Step 4: Enjoy! ☺☻

Breakfast Smoothie

Description

A breakfast smoothie is a healthy and fruity way to start the day. It is such a popular drink that restaurants/bars opened up just to make smoothies!

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Frozen Berries
  • Peanut Butter
  • Almond

Steps:

  • Step 1: Grab a banana, frozen berries, peanut butter, and almond milk.
  • Step 2: Put 1 cup of frozen berries, 1 banana, 1 spoonful of peanut butter, and half a cup of almond milk into a blender.
  • Step 3: Blend for 30 seconds or until it is liquid enough to drink. If it is too thick, add more milk and reblend.
  • Step 4: Enjoy! ☺☻

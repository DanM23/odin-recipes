Flaming Hot Peanut Butter Sandwich

  • Step 1: Put peanut butter on both pieces of bread.
  • Step 2: Lay Flaming Hot Cheetos vertically on the peanut butter side of the bread slices.
  • Step 3: Place both slices together with the Cheeto side facing inward towards eachother and press to make sure it stays together
  • Step 4: Enjoy! ☺☻

Flaming Hot Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The flamming hot peanut butter and jelly sandwich sounds like something that was invented by a hungover student in a college dorm with the last ingredients left in the cabinet, and it was

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread
  • Flaming Hot Cheetos

Steps:

  • Step 1: Put peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  • Step 2: Put Jelly on the other piece of bread.
  • Step 3: Lay Flaming Hot Cheetos vertically on the peanut butter side and the jelly side of the bread slices.
  • Step 4: Place both slices together with the cheeto sides facing inward towards eachother and press to make sure it stays together.
  • Step 5: Enjoy! ☺☻

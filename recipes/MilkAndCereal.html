Milk and Cereal

Description

Milk and cereal is a classic staple to every breakfast around the globe. It is a meal you start eating as a child and never stop until your death bed. It is truly a one of a kind, simple meal.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal
  • Bowl

Steps:

  • Step 1: Grab a bowl, cereal, and your favorite kind of milk.
  • Step 2: Pour the cereal into the bowl.
  • Step 3: Pour the milk in so it covers 4/5ths of the cereal.
  • Step 4: Enjoy! ☺☻

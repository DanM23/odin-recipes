Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a healthy way to start the morning. It is dense which will help keep you full for until you get around to lunch.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Hot Water
  • Bowl

Steps:

  • Step 1: Grab a packet of Quaker Oats oatmeal and a bowl.
  • Step 2: Fill bowl with oatmeal, and fill with hot, almost boiling, water and let it sit for several minutes.
  • Step 3: Stir occasionally while it is soaking in the water so it mixes evenly.
  • Step 4: Let is sit for another couple minutes to cool and then it will be safe to eat.
  • Step 5: Enjoy! ☺☻
